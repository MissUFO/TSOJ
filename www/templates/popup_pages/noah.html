Noah

Old Testament figure who lived after Adam and before Abraham, probably about 2500 years before Christ.

In Noah's time, people became so wicked that God sent a flood to kill them all. God warned Noah of the flood, and he believed God even though his neighbors made fun of him for building a huge boat in the middle of the desert.

The whole earth was covered with water. When it began subsiding, Noah and his wife and his extended family came upon dry land.

God made the rainbow as a promise to Noah and his descendants that he would never send another worldwide flood.
