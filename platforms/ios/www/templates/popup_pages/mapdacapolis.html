Decapolis

It was a confederation of ten Hellenistic cities formed after Pompey took control of the area about 65 years before Christ was born. Most of the people in this region were Gentiles (anyone who wasn't a Jew was a Gentile), and many of them were Greek-speaking Roman soldiers. The cities had banded together to protect their citizens from Arabian tribes and militant Jews.
