Jerusalem

Already more than a thousand years old at the time of Jesus, the city remains venerated as the home of the Temple built by Solomon, son of King David.

At the time of Jesus, Jerusalem was in control of the Roman-backed army of Herod, having been under both Persian and Greek control in previous centuries.

Located on the heights overlooking the Rift valley, at 2500 feet above sea level, it is not uncommon for it to snow, allowing for hot summer days but cool evenings.
