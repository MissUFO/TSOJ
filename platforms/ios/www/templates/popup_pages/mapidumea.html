Idumea

A province to the south of Judea. Also known as Edom. The people were descendants of Jacob's brother Esau.

Jacob and Esau's father was Isaac, Abraham's son, so they were Abraham's grandsons. Jacob and Esau were twins and were trying to get the best of each other their whole lives.
