Psalm

A lyrical poem or prayer.

Some were written to go with music so people could sing them. Their purpose is to praise God.

Many were written by David and express not only his reverence and awe of God, but also his emotions when he was going through difficult times in his life -- when he was being treated unfairly, when people were trying to kill him, and when he was repenting for sins he had committed (murder, adultery).

In the Bible, the book of Psalms is a collection of 150 of these psalms.
