Sea of Galilee at Sunrise

Sea of Galilee

Many of Jesus' disciples were fishermen on the Sea of Galilee. The lake was known for violent squalls that would come up with little warning.

Modern fisherman on the Sea of Galilee

One of the main industries was salting the fish to be exported across the Roman Empire. When Jesus was talking about the "salt of the earth" in Matthew 5: 13, the people from that area knew about salt because it was their job, and they would see it every day.
