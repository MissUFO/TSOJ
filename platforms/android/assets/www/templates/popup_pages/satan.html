Satan/Beelzebub

These are two names for the same thing. Satan was the highest angel in heaven when the universe was created.

He wanted to usurp God, and because of his sin of pride, he was thrown out of heaven along with a third of the angels who chose to follow him.

He is opposed to God and tried without success to get Jesus to go against God when Jesus was on earth. Beelzebub comes from a word that means "lord of flies," or "lord or dung."
